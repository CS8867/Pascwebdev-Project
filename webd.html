About Us

Thousands of people suffer because local hospitals & clinics don’t have the medical supplies and equipment needed to save lives in their communities. Children are missing the chance to get an education and live a full life due to illnesses that would be treatable if they had access to proper health services.

Aarogya Foundation aims at providing all the necessary assistance and help to the needy in this aspect. We do this by sending out certified doctors and nurses as volunteers, and also have tie-ups with some of the best urban hospitals, which provide healthcare at reduced rates to people below the poverty line.

Arogya Foundation works primarily in India and some regions of Nepal and Bangladesh.

Covid-19 Relief

Aarogya is working in overdrive to get protective gear and critical care medications to as many health workers as possible, as quickly as possible, with emergency deliveries leaving daily for medical facilities across the Country.

Aarogya is coordinating with public health authorities, nonprofit organizations and businesses in India to provide personal protective equipment and essential medical items to health workers responding to coronavirus (COVID-19).
